CoMotion 2020

Website for CoMotion 2020

link to site

Featured on One Page Love.

Role: Visual design and Front-end development, working with Eric Flatt

I was given the oppurtunity from the CoMotion branding team to design and develop the website for CoMotion 2020, a student-led motion graphics conference at SCAD. My task was to not only display all the information of the event in a clear and seemless way, but also to envision the style and narrative of the event through the medium of a website as well. The website is used by hundreds of motion media students and employers alike, and documents and credits all the hard work put into this event.
