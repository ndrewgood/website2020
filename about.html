about

My name is Andrew Goodridge and I'm a student at SCAD studying User Experience Design. I have a passion for creating memorable and delightful digital experiences, while being both seamless and accessible.

Blending technology with art and design has always been a strong interest of mine, and is something I've kept close to heart for as long as I've had access to a computer. I have a very holistic approach to understanding digital products, which is also why I have a strong interest in developing and motion graphics.

In addition to design, music has always had a major influence on myself, whether it be through listening or performing. I'm also always open to new conversations and new people, professional or casual. Feel free to email or DM through any of my social medias. ✌️
